Voltaire (François-Marie Arouet)

Life of Voltaire:

  • 1694 - Born in Paris, France.
  • 1704 - Enrolled at College Louis-le-Grand, a Jesuit secondary school.
  • 1716 - Exiled to Tulle.
  • 1759 - Published the novella, Candide, widely regarded as his magnum opus.
  • 1778 - Died at the age of 83.
